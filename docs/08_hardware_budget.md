# Hardware budget

Suggested planning:
- minimum practical: 24 GB GPU, 64 GB RAM, 1 TB NVMe
- comfortable: 48 GB GPU, 96–128 GB RAM, 2 TB NVMe
- strong: 80 GB GPU or equivalent, 128 GB RAM, 2 TB+ NVMe

Also reserve storage for:
- raw dataset
- extracted dataset
- processed exports
- checkpoints
- logs
